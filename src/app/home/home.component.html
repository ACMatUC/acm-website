ACM@UC

We're the student chapter of the Association of Computing Machinery at the University of Cincinnati. ACM@UC is open to all majors and acm.org membership is not required to participate.

Meetings

Unless stated otherwise, ACM@UC meetings are held on alternating Thursdays at 6:00pm at room 601 in Swift Hall. Consult a map of UC to find Swift Hall if you are not familiar with its location on campus.

We have great speakers come in for our meetings and give tech talks and workshops to help the computing community at the University of Cincinnati grow.
