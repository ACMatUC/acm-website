We're the student chapter of the Association of Computing Machinery at the University of Cincinnati. ACM@UC is open to all majors and acm.org membership is not required to participate.

Meetings

Unless stated otherwise, ACM@UC meetings are held on alternating Thursdays at 6:00pm at Campus Rec Center 3220. Consult a campus map to find the Campus Rec Center if you are not familiar with the area. For more information about meetings and other ACM events, check the calendar below.

We have great speakers come in for our meetings and give tech talks and workshops to help the computing community at the University of Cincinnati grow.

See acmatuc/archive on GitHub for links to slides and other resources from our meetings.
