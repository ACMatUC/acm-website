Home

We're the student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery at the University of Cincinnati. ACM@UC is open to all majors and official ACM membership is not required to participate.

Getting Involved

Join us on Slack to stay up to date with the ACM@UC community. Subscribe to ACM@UC on the UC Listserv, join our page on CampusLINK, follow @ACMatUCorg on Twitter or on LinkedIn, or join our group on Facebook for meeting alerts.

Office Hours

Have questions on homework? Want help reviewing for a test? Want to talk programming? Show off your project? Come on down to our office hours every Tuesday from 3pm to 4pm and Wednesday from 6pm to 7pm. Our office is located in Rhodes 802A (look for the glass “fish bowl” near collaboration zone lobby).

Contact

Got a question or an idea? Talk to us in Slack or send us an email.
