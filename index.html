Home

Interested in ICPC? Fill out the form here to participate in October.

We're the student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery at the University of Cincinnati. ACM@UC is open to all majors and acm.org membership is not required to participate.

Getting Involved

Join us on Slack to stay up to date with the ACM@UC community. Subscribe to ACM@UC on the UC Listserv, follow @ACMatUCorg on Twitter, or join our group on Facebook for meeting alerts.

Office Hours

Have questions on homework? Want help reviewing for a test? Want to talk programming? Show off your project? Come on down to our office hours every Monday from 6pm to 7pm and Thursday from 1pm to 2pm. Our office is located in Rhodes 802A (look for the glass “fish bowl” near collaboration zone lobby).

Contact

Got a question or an idea? Talk to us in Slack or send us an email.
